[Floriane Odje](https://www.linkedin.com/in/floriane-odje/?locale=en_US) is a PhD student at [Saarland University](https://www.uni-saarland.de/en/home.html), Germany, where she works in the [Volkamer Lab](https://volkamerlab.org) as part of the [MORPHEUS project](https://volkamerlab.org/projects/morpheus/). Her research focuses on identifying endocrine activity patterns from Cell Painting data and integrating morphological and chemical information to predict endocrine disruption.
